What is Online Sikkim Manipal University?

Online Sikkim Manipal University (MUJ Online) is a UGC-entitled university offering globally recognized online degree programs. It provides undergraduate and postgraduate courses that combine academic excellence with digital convenience for students and working professionals.

Are the online degrees from Sikkim Manipal University valid and recognized?

Yes. All online programs offered by Sikkim Manipal University are UGC-entitled and AICTE-approved (where applicable). The degrees are recognized at par with regular on-campus degrees for employment and higher education in India and abroad.

What courses are offered under Online Sikkim Manipal University?

MUJ Online offers a range of programs such as BBA, BCA, MBA, MCA, M.Com, and MA in Journalism & Mass Communication. Each course is designed with an industry-aligned curriculum and flexible online learning.

How are classes conducted for online programs?

All classes are delivered through Online Manipal‚Äôs digital learning platform, which includes live sessions, recorded lectures, e-books, discussion forums, and assessments. Students can learn anytime, anywhere using mobile, tablet, or laptop.

Is there placement or career support available for online students?

Yes. MUJ Online offers career guidance, resume-building sessions, mock interviews, and virtual placement drives. Students also gain access to Manipal‚Äôs extensive alumni network and industry connections for better opportunities.
