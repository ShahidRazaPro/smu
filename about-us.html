ABOUT SMU-DE

Sikkim Manipal University Distance Education SMU-DE was established in a partnership between the Government of Sikkim and Manipal Group in 1995. SMU-DE has won many awards including student’s most preferred distance learning University. The University offers various Bachelors and Masters Programmes in Information technology and management studies. All the courses of SMU-DE are approved by various regulatory bodies like Medical Council of India, Nursing Institutes of India, AICTE, UGC, and DEB.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

A UGC-entitled university offering flexible, accredited online degree programs for students and working professionals.

Where is SMU-DE situated?

Sikkim Manipal University Distance Education was established in 1995 & is situated in Sikkim Gangtok.

Is SMU-DE approved by UGC?

Yes, SMU-DE courses are approved by the UGC-DEB

Is SMU distance education degree valid?

Yes, a degree obtained from SMU-DE is valid as university is recognized by the (UGC) – Distance Education Bureau (DEB).

Is it worth to do a distance course from SMU?

Yes, it is worth to do distance course from SMU because it is approved by UGC-DEB. Also, working professional gets a platform to continue their study through distance platform along with their job

How can I apply for SMU distance learning MBA program?

Sikkim Manipal University invites application twice in a year for the admission. Students looking for admission may apply online on the website.
