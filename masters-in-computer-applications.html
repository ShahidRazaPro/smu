Overview of SMU Online MCA Course

Sikkim Manipal University (SMU) offers Online MCA (Master of Computer Applications) courses for imparting advanced computer applications and information technology skills among students. The program emphasizes an all-inclusive curriculum that combines both theoretical understanding and practical, making sure its graduates are geared up to enter the technical industry. Students are given opportunities to attend online interactive classes, project work in the industry, and customized mentoring. The adaptive learning platform, through its effectiveness, helps the students to multitask with minimal extra time, hence, a very perfect spin for the working professionals in need of upgrading their vocational and professional opportunities. Additionally, the inculcation of the spirit of innovation and research into the course enables the students to acquire the critical thinking and problem-solving skills that are vital for the success of the contemporary IT competition. Join now for a change in the way you perceive the future of technology through SMU.

Sikkim Manipal University's online MCA program is a two-year degree program that provides a deep understanding of various computer applications aspects, including programming, software development, database management, network management, and more. The program is designed to provide students with a complete understanding of computer applications and skills required to develop and manage complex computer systems.

The Online MCA course is delivered through online learning platforms, which includes video lectures, online discussions, and virtual classrooms. Students have the benefit to learn the online MCA program from anywhere at their own convenience.

  • Eligibility: Candidate must have 3 Year Bachelor's Degree from recognized University with Mathematics at 10+2 or at Graduation Level.
  • Semester Fee: 24,500 (inclusive of exam fee 5,000/-), or as revised time to time.
  • Total Course Fee: INR 98,000/-

Who May Apply and Indian & International Students Eligibility Requirements

Sikkim Manipal University (SMU) offers an Online MBA program for Indian and international students who want to enhance their professional careers with an industry-focused, flexible management degree. The eligibility requirements are outlined below:

Indian Students

  • Must possess a bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, engineering, or a related field from a recognized university.
  • Should have the minimum aggregate percentage requirements as defined by SMU.
  • Applicants with a science or engineering background but with a keen interest in computer applications are also invited to apply.
  • Other tests like entrance tests or interviews may be included in the selection process.

International Students

  • Should hold an equivalent bachelor's degree from a recognized university in their home country.
  • Should submit evidence of English language proficiency (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL) if their previous studies were not in English.
  • Documents required are academic transcripts, identity proofs, and work experience certificates related to the field of study.

SMU Online MCA Course Fee Structure

Sikkim Manipal University Online MCA fees is ₹98,000 (₹24,500 per semester). Payment can be made either semester-wise, annually, or as a lump sum. EMI schemes costing ₹4,083 per month and no-cost EMI schemes both have a 24-month tenure.
Scholarship privileges include 30% for individuals from Sikkim & Northeast and 20% for each of the Defense, Divyang, and alumni categories. An application fee of ₹500 is applicable, whereas the exam fee is bundled with the semester fee.

Admission Process for SMU Online MCA Course

The entire procedure for SMU Online MCA course admission is digital and convenient for applicants. The first step for the aspirants is filling out the registration form on the SMU website, making sure that they fill in all the personal and academic details correctly. After that, candidates need to be prepared to submit their documents such as academic transcripts, certificates, and a valid ID as the next step. The admissions team will screen the received applications to ensure that the applicants meet the criteria and that the provided documents are genuine.
