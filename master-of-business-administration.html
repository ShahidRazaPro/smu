Who May Apply and Indian & International Students Eligibility Requirements

Sikkim Manipal University (SMU) offers an Online MBA program for Indian and international students who want to enhance their professional careers with an industry-focused, flexible management degree. The eligibility requirements are outlined below:

Indian Students

  • A bachelor's degree from a well-recognized university in any discipline.
  • A minimum aggregate percentage following SMU admission guidelines.
  • No entrance test is necessary; however, the candidates can be required to pass a university-level selection process.
  • Work experience (if any) can be taken into consideration for some specializations.

International Students

  • A bachelor's degree or an equivalent qualification from a university that is affiliated with it.
  • Proof of English language skills (IELTS, TOEFL, or the like) if the previous degree was not in an English-speaking country.
  • Satisfying the visa and document verification requirements per Indian education standards.
  • As potential students should be aware of the fact that admission requirements may change, the official SMU website or the university should be visited or contacted for the latest information.

Admission Process for SMU Online MBA Course

The admissions process for SMU Online MBA is simple and entirely online. Begin by going to the official website of SMU and filling out the online registration form with correct personal and academic information. Then, upload documents such as academic transcripts, degree certificates, and proof of identity. The admissions staff goes through your application to ensure eligibility and authenticity of documents. After approval, you will be issued an offer letter and fee payment instructions. You can either pay the fees in full or in instalments. Lastly, get your confirmation email, which opens the online learning platform and course materials to you.
