Online Bachelor of Computer Application {BCA}

SMU Online/Distance BCA:-

Sikkim Manipal University BCA programs help aspirants to pursue their 3 years (6 semesters) under graduation in BCA while pursuing their career.

Online/Distance BCA programme from Sikkim Manipal University (SMU) is designed to provide students with a complete education in computer science, including courses in programming languages, database management, software development, web development, and networking. These courses are taught through a combination of online lectures, video tutorials, interactive assignments, and group projects.

One of the major benefits of online BCA program from Sikkim Manipal University is that students can complete the program entirely online, which allows them to study from anywhere in the world, & at any time.

Eligibility: Candidate must have completed 10+2 or equivalent / 3 Year Diploma from a recognized board.

Semester Fee: 16,500 (inclusive of exam fee 5,000/-), or as revised time to time.
